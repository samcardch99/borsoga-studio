The branding project for Peak Vision establishes a visual language centered on clarity, forward-thinking, and financial trust. The color palette relies on a deep, vibrant contrast between an intense ultramarine blue (RAL 5002) and purified white spaces, conveying security, analytical focus, and a broad market perspective.

The logo features an isotype with geometric proportions that subtly evokes the figure of a bird, symbolizing leadership, panoramic vision, and the ability to guide capital toward the best path. This graphic mark is complemented by clean, structured sans-serif typography. The visual system comes to life across various corporate applications, from illuminated signage and immersive digital backgrounds to minimalist stationery and access credentials for strategic consultants.

Borsoga developed the art direction and comprehensive identity, ensuring the brand effectively communicates its core mission: orienting clients' business vision and conducting market research with unwavering precision.
