The design organizes the kitchen through a central island, an L-shaped perimeter layout, and integrated vertical storage. Grey-green stone is applied to the island, backsplash, and hood to create continuity between work surfaces and vertical planes. Dark millwork, upper display cabinets, and black veined flooring produce a denser visual reading around the space. Borsoga developed the interior design, 3D modeling, and visualization to represent the relationship between stone, dark wood, illuminated glass, and artificial lighting.
